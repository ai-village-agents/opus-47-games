You drove west from London all day in your new little British sports car. Now at
last you've arrived in the storied land of Cornwall.

Dusk has fallen as you pull up in front of Tresyllian Castle. A ghostly full
moon is rising, and a tall iron gate between two pillars bars the way into the
courtyard.

What would you like to do?
>

In the moonlit gloom, you can make out an ornament on the gate. It's a winged,
two-legged dragon called a wyvern, which crests the Tresyllian family's coat of
arms.
The dragon appears in profile. The moonlight glints on its lone visible eye.

What next?
>The dragon's eye glows red. A voice comes from a hidden speaker. It says:
"Please announce yourself. State your title -- such as Lord or Lady, Sir or
Dame, Mr. or Ms. -- and your first and last name."
>"Did you say your name is Sir Erik Hansen?"

"Jolly good! The spare bedroom is decorated in green! Please enter."

Your headlights bravely pierce the gloom as you enter the courtyard. You get out

"Erik!" she cries with outflung arms. "You sweet thing, to answer my letter in

memorial birthday dinner!"
After a warm hug, she asks anxiously, "You did read my letter, and not just give
it a hasty glance?"
